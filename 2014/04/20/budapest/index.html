Budapest

20 Apr 2014

Budapest is the capital of Hungary.

Process

As it turns out, the name ‘Budapest’ is a portmanteau of the two towns ‘Buda’ and ‘Pest’, split by the river Danube, that were united several hundred years ago.

After initially toying around with using the middle of the B to represent the river separating two towns, I realised that B and P could be made to be part of a larger B.

Futura seemed an apt typeface to start from as it’s European but with a less severe character than the Swiss Helvetica.
